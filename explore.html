✦ Practical Guide

Before You Explore

✈️

Getting There

Fly Kathmandu → Dhangadhi → Dipayal Airport. Or overnight bus Kathmandu → Dhangadhi, then local bus to Silgadhi. Hotel Danfe offers airport transfer service.

🌤️

Best Season

October–November: crisp skies, peak trekking. March–April: blooming rhododendrons. Avoid July–August (heavy monsoon). December–February: snow-dusted and breathtaking.

🎒

What to Pack

Layered warm clothing, trekking shoes, sunscreen, rain jacket (monsoon) and a camera. Cash is recommended as ATMs are limited in remote areas.

🗺️

Local Transport

Jeeps and local buses connect Silgadhi to most destinations. For Khaptad, helicopter charters from Doti Airport are available. Our trekking desk arranges everything.

🍽️

Local Cuisine

Try Dhido, Gundruk soup and Sel Roti. Our restaurant serves authentic Doteli cuisine. Teahouses along trekking routes offer hearty meals and warm Nepali hospitality.

📞

Need Help?

Our team at Hotel Danfe is your local expert. Call +977 9848431439 for travel advice, permits, guided tours and emergency assistance.
