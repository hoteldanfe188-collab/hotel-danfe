✦ Our Story

A Sanctuary in
the Hills of Doti

Nestled in Silgadhi — the historic capital of the ancient Doti Kingdom — Hotel Danfe offers a warm, comfortable retreat for travellers exploring Nepal's enchanting Far-Western frontier.

We take our name from the Danfe, the vibrant Himalayan Monal pheasant and Nepal's national bird, symbolising the natural splendour and cultural pride of this remarkable region.

Whether visiting the sacred Shaileshwari Temple, trekking Khaptad National Park, or discovering the Seti River valleys — Hotel Danfe is your ideal base in Dipayal Silgadhi.

✦ Explore Doti

Discover the Wonders
Around You

01

Shaileshwari Temple

The most revered shrine in Far-Western Nepal — dedicated to Goddess Shaileshwari, just 5 minutes from the hotel.

02

Khaptad National Park

Pristine sanctuary at 3,000–3,700m. Helicopter access from Doti Airport. Known for meadows and spiritual heritage.

03

Seti River Valley

Milky glacial river winding through Doti's valleys — serene walks, photography and pure natural beauty.

04

Doti Kingdom Ruins

Remnants of Nepal's ancient Doti Kingdom — fort, temples and inscriptions tell a fascinating medieval story.

05

Gurukhola Dham

Tranquil pilgrimage site of spiritual significance set amid natural beauty and peaceful birdsong.
