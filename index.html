✦   Our Story

A Sanctuary in
the Hills of Doti

Nestled in Silgadhi — the historic capital of the ancient Doti Kingdom — Hotel Danfe offers a warm and comfortable retreat for travellers exploring Nepal's enchanting Far-Western frontier.

We take our name from the Danfe, the vibrant Himalayan Monal pheasant and Nepal's national bird, symbolising the natural splendour and cultural pride of this remarkable region.

Whether you are visiting the sacred Shaileshwari Temple, trekking through Khaptad National Park, or discovering the valleys along the Seti River, Hotel Danfe is your ideal base in Dipayal Silgadhi.

✦   Explore Doti

Discover the Wonders
Around You

01

Shaileshwari Temple

One of the most revered temples in Far-Western Nepal, dedicated to Goddess Shaileshwari — a manifestation of Bhagwati/Durga. A deeply sacred site for the people of Doti, right in the heart of Silgadhi.

02

Khaptad National Park

A pristine sanctuary spanning four districts, celebrated for its stunning landscapes, rich biodiversity, and the meditative legacy of the Hindu sage Khaptad Baba. Accessible by helicopter from Doti Airport.

03

Seti River Valley

The beautiful Seti River winds through Doti's valleys, offering serene walks, photography, and peaceful connection with nature. A favourite of hikers and nature lovers.

04

Doti Kingdom Ruins

Explore remnants of the ancient Doti Kingdom — one of Nepal's eight medieval principalities. The Doti Killa (fort) and historical temples offer a window into the region's remarkable past.

05

Gurukhola Dham

A tranquil religious site known for its peaceful atmosphere and spiritual significance. Attracts devoted pilgrims and visitors seeking reflection amid natural beauty.
